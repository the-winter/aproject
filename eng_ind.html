Red Peonies (Crveni Božuri, pronounced Tsrvenee Bozhuree) is an amateur a cappella choir in Perth, Western Australia, primarily performing music that defines the Serbian heritage. Our repertoire includes a wide range of music spanning sacred and Liturgic, traditional, folk, ethno and modern genres. We also enjoy performing music from other nations within the Balkans and beyond.

Red Peonies was founded in January 2016 and is led by Jasna and Nada. It began as a dual harmony female choir and has since expanded to include two Bass vocalists. We are still missing tenors and would be delighted if anyone would be interested to join. Speaking Serbian is not a requirement, and there will be plenty of help with pronounciation.

We regularly perform at various events in the Serbian community, and in the coming year will begin performing for the Australian public as well.
